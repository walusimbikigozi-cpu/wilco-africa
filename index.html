About
2010

Built for Africa's
Industrial Reality

Wilco Africa Group Ltd is a limited liability company registered in Kenya. Since 2010, we have operated across East and Central Africa — building the infrastructure that keeps industrial assets safe, operational, and compliant.

We are pioneers in integrated industrial protection: the only firm in the region combining Muay Thai-trained manned guarding, certified K9 units, perimeter hardening, fire systems, cross-border logistics, and industrial corrosion control under one operational command.

In 2026, Wilco expanded into industrial asset management for Africa's factories and plants — extending our mandate from protection to the full asset lifecycle.

Company Timeline
2010
Founded in Kenya. Operations begin across Nairobi and Malindi with manned guarding and K9 services.
2013
Expansion into Uganda — Kampala, Entebbe, Makerere. Cross-border logistics mandate established.
2016
DRC border documentation operations at Malaba. Aerial transport route mapping across East/Central Africa.
2019
Partnerships with Opel Auto, ATES Uganda, Macmedia Africa Ltd. Bunia operations active.
2023
Electric fencing, Hi-Speed gates, and full fire alarm systems added to service portfolio.
2026
Industrial asset management division launched. First deployment: Mzee Mike Fertilizer Plant, Ngong.
